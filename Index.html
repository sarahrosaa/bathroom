What are Bathrooms

A bathroom is a room that has 4 main parts. The 4 main parts are the toilet, the tub, the shower, and the sink. Bathrooms that only have a toilet and a sink are known as 'half baths'. This is because they only have two of the four components needed to be a full bathroom.
